PROJECT SNOWFLAKE

Project Snowflake is a game heavily influenced by the dark humor and action of the animated show, Murder Drones by GLITCH.

While the game adopts the show's unique animation style, it carves its own path with a completely original story and cast set in a desolate post-apocalyptic world reminiscent of Murder Drones' Copper-9.
